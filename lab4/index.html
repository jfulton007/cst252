Task 1: How to brush your teeth properly. 1. Wet your toothbrush with sink water. 2. Put toothpaste on your toothbrush. 3. Start brushing slowly and carefully, making sure to get harder to reach areas and your lower gum line. 4. After doing this for three minutes, spit out everything and rinse your mouth out with mouthrinse or water. You’re ready to start eating & repeat the process later that night! Task 2: How to install a Steam game. 1. Make sure you have Steam download & set up on your computer. You will also need a registered Steam account to complete this tutorial. 2. Hover over the Library section of the Steam application, it is on the upper left, and then click on “Home”. 3. Click on which game you’d like to install, then hit the bright blue “INSTALL” button. 4. Grant the necessary permission to the program, wait for the download to complete, let the game install whatever software it needs - and you’re done! The game is ready to play.
